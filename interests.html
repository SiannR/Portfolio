  • Brain Bee

    I participated in the Year 11 Brain Bee competition, where I gained valuable knowledge about the brain and neuroscience. I advanced through round one and had the chance to visit Auckland University. There, I met scientists and learned more about the fascinating work they do on the brain. It was an incredible, once-in-a-lifetime experience that deepened my interest in neuroscience.
